What is the WWCC

Working Women Community Center is a charitable organization that provides immigrant women with opportunities to improve the quality of their lives of those in their families and community.

Established in 1976, Working Women has grown to provide programs and services over 25 languages throughout five locations across the city of Toronto, including two high priority neighbourhoods. Although our programs and services vary. At the core of all we do is one constanr goal to empower immigrant women to do more and be more within their family and community

Whether we are helping immigrant women retain for a job. Encouraging them to take an active role in their children's educational success; or allowing them to inspire other immigrant women realize their full potential, Working Women shows immigrant women that they have the ability to live a fully productive and engage life and that doing so has a positive effect on those around them
